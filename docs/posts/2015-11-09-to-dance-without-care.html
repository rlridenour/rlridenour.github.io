To Dance Without Care

Nov 09, 2015 00:00

Lord Jesus,

You invite us
to lay down our
burdens, cares, concerns,
fears, and anxieties,
and give them all to you.

Then, wrapped in the
sweet warmth of your grace,
we can dance in joy
before the throne,
as beloved children
of the Father
who have finally
found their way home.

Amen
