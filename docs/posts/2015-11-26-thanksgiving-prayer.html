Thanksgiving Prayer

Nov 26, 2015 00:00

Gracious Father,

There are those today
who struggle to give thanks
because of loss,
illness,
separation,
or grief.

May their spirits
be comforted with
the knowledge that
our thoughts and prayers
are with them today.

Amen
