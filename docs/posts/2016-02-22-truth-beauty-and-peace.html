Truth, Beauty, and Peace

Feb 22, 2016

Father,

There are days
when it seems
like I’m creating
a minefield with
carefully placed
hazards of
discord,
ugliness,
and lies.

Help me instead
to plant seeds of
truth,
beauty,
and peace;
building a place
where others can
run without care —

Barefoot,
with joy,
in the fields
of the Lord.
