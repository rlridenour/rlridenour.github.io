To Love People As They Are

Jun 29, 2016

God of grace,

You stand and knock,
not demanding that we come to you,
asking only that we open our hearts
that you may come to us.

Help me, likewise,
to love others just as
you have loved me —

Where they are,
and as they are,
not how I want
them to be.

Amen
