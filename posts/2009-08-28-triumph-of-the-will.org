#+title: Triumph of the Will

/Triumph of the Will/, directed by Leni Riefenstahl, is the film that I couldn't remember in Aesthetics this morning. Roger Ebert, in his 1994 [[http://rogerebert.suntimes.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/19940624/REVIEWS/406240302/1023][review]] of a documentary about Riefenstahl asks exactly the right question:
    
#+begin_quote
"These are by general consent two of the best documentaries ever made. But because they reflect the ideology of a monstrous movement, they pose a classic question of the contest between art and morality: Is there such a thing as pure art, or does all art make a political statement?"
#+end_quote
