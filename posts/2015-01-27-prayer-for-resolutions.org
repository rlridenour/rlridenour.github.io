#+title: Prayer for Resolutions

Gracious God,

I resolve\\
to laugh,\\
to listen,\\
and to love\\
like one whose\\
eyes have just\\
been opened in\\
joy and wonder.

In the name of\\
the one who\\
makes wine from water\\
and beauty from ashes,

/Amen/
