#+title: Maundy Thursday 2015

Lord,

How would you have us known to the world?

By whom we exclude?\\
By what we hate?\\
By whom we refuse to serve?\\
By our purity?

Instead, we are to be known\\
by our love...

Love,

in all its\\
messy,\\
entangled,\\
accepting,\\
and sometimes awkward\\
beauty.

/Amen/
