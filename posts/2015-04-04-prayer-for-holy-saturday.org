#+title: Prayer for Holy Saturday

Was creation in despair\\
on that single day in history\\
when Nietzsche's word\\
rang true?

The tomb is sealed,\\
the body is cold,\\
and heaven mourns\\
at what we have done.

God is dead,\\
and we have killed him.

Lord, have mercy.

/Amen/
