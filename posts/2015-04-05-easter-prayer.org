#+title: Easter Prayer

Prayer for a rainy Easter Day:

The refreshing rain\\
falls upon the parched land\\
And brings life to the\\
cracked, barren earth.

In the same way, may the\\
Spirit of the risen Christ\\
heal our broken hearts and\\
restore our barren souls.

/Amen/
