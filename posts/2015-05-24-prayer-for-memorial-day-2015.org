#+title: Prayer for Memorial Day, 2015


Holy God,

We give thanks today,\\
not for those who died,

but for those who lived\\
in such a way that they\\
witnessed to a commitment\\
to something worth\\
sacrificing for,

something greater than\\
even their own lives.

*Amen*
