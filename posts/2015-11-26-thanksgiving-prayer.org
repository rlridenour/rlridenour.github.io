#+title: Thanksgiving Prayer

Gracious Father,

There are those today\\
who struggle to give thanks\\
because of loss,\\
illness,\\
separation,\\
or grief.

May their spirits\\
be comforted with\\
the knowledge that\\
our thoughts and prayers\\
are with them today.

/Amen/
