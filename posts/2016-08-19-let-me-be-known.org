#+title: Let Me Be Known

Lord,

Let me be known,

not by what I hate,\\
but by whom I love;

not by whom I exclude,\\
but by whom I include;

not by my enemies,\\
but by my friends;

for an enemy truly loved\\
is but a friend to be.


/Amen/
