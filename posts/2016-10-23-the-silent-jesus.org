#+title: The Silent Jesus

God of Grace,

When Jesus was offered\\
the choice to be used\\
or to be silent,

He chose silence.

May I guard against\\
the temptation to craft\\
an image of Jesus to use\\
for my own political\\
and personal use.

Thy will, not my own, Lord.

/Amen/
