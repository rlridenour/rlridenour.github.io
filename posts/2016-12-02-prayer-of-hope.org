#+title: Prayer of Hope

God of Hope,

Advent begins with\\
a single, small flame\\
on a solitary candle,\\
a gentle promise\\
that We are not alone.

Here is our hope,\\
the darkness will pass,\\
for the light is coming.

/Amen/
