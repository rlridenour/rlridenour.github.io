#+title: Prayer for the New Year

Lord,

May I not confuse

Naive, blind optimism\\
with true hope,

Self-righteous purity\\
with true holiness,

The voice of my own desires\\
with the voice of the Lord,

Or my own country\\
with the Kingdom of God.

/Amen/
