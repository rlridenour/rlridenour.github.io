#+title: Prayer for the Silenced

Am I so wise\\
that I have nothing\\
left to learn,\\
or just afraid of\\
what I might hear?

Though others cannot,\\
the voice of God still speaks,\\
warning that justice comes,\\
both for me\\
and for those\\
I have silenced.

/Amen/
