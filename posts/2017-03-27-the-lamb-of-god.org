(Painting: /Lamb/ by Francisco Zurbarán)[fn:1]

The sounds of Passover\\
fill the streets with\\
desperate bleating\\
as all the animals\\
must be dragged\\
to play their\\
sacrificial roles.

All, that is, save one\\
who goes quietly\\
with determination,\\
strength, and purpose.

Lamb of God\\
have mercy on me.

/Amen/

[fn:1] Zurbarán, Francisco, 1598-1664. /Lamb/, from Art in the Christian Tradition, a project of the Vanderbilt Divinity Library, Nashville, TN. <http://diglib.library.vanderbilt.edu/act-imagelink.pl?RC=47446> [retrieved March 27, 2017]. Original source: <www.yorckproject.de>.
