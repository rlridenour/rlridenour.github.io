#+title: Prayer for Holy Saturday

Loving God,

Despair or hope?

That was the choice\\
on that day when\\
all seemed lost.

Though it may\\
seem foolish,\\
we choose hope,\\
and wait to see\\
what tomorrow brings.

/Amen/
