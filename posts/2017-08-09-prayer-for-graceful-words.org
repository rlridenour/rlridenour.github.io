#+title: "Prayer for Graceful Words"
#+filetags: prayer religion
#+date: <2017-08-09>

Holy God,

In my haste to be heard,\\
give me the strength\\
to slow down,\\
think carefully,\\
and measure\\
my words.

Some words are poisonous fruit,\\
and others are seeds of grace.

Help me to sow\\
according to your will.

/Amen/
