#+title: Be the Body of Christ
#+filetags: prayer religion
#+date: <2017-09-12>

We are the Body of Christ.

He cannot go where\\
we refuse to tread,\\
speak what we\\
refuse to say,\\
or share what\\
we refuse to give.

May we be the\\
instruments of\\
his love and grace.

/Amen/
