#+title: In Offense, Giving Thanks
#+filetags: prayer religion
#+date: <2017-09-30>

Holy God,

Thank you\\
for the one\\
who offends me,\\
for in offense\\
my heart is laid bare\\
and my idols are revealed.

My anger declares\\
the object of my worship.

Is it myself,\\
the empire,\\
or you?

Lord, have mercy.
